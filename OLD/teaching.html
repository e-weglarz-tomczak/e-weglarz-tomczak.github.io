Ewelina Węglarz-Tomczak

Medicinal chemist, biochemist, systems biologist, and drug designer by trade and nature-lover at heart, with a Ph.D. with Honours from Department of Bioorganic Chemistry at PWr Wroclaw, Postdoc from Swammerdam Institute for Life Sciences at the UvA Amsterdam. Also educated at Ruhr Universitat Bochum, Jagiellonian University Medical College, and Institute of Immunology and Experimental Therapy PAN.
The founder and CSO at a R&D based biopharmaceutical company NatInLab.
Interested in creating novel hi-tech solutions for the development of innovative medicines and new (bio)technologies.
Passionate about horses, show-jumping amateur aiming the Olympic games.
