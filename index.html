Ewelina Węglarz-Tomczak

I am a postdoc in Swammerdam Institute for Life Sciences at the University of Amsterdam. Previously, I was a postdoc in Department of Bioorganic Chemistry at Wroclaw University of Science and Technology. My research interest focus on network-based study of metabolism with the aim of understanding its complexity and find out effective therapy. It concerns the study of metabolic abnormalities in cancer cells, such as increased glycolysis. My work integtate analytical chemistry, cancer biology and network modeling.
